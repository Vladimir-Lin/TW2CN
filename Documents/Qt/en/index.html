TW2CN

TW2CN is a small too for converting Traditional Chinese HTML files into Simplified Chinese.


TW2CN --all

Convert every html files in the directory from Traditional Chinese into Simplified Chinese.

TW2CN --files file1 file2 file3 ...

Convert specific files from Traditional Chinese into Simplified Chinese.

TW2CN will search for Replacements.txt in the local directory. The format is, first one is Traditional Chinese, the second one is Simplified Chinese.
